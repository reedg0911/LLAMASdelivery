Move that mountain, for wellness, with Llamas

I grew up in a household that believed in a healthy diet and exercise, but did not think much of it. As I grew older, I took notice of the diet an average person consumes, with unnatural additives, unnecessary corn products, and artificial sweeteners. Heck, even so called “natural” food has been biologically engineered for the masses. As members of my life, friends, and family, convert to a vegan or vegetarian diet I’ve begun to really divulge. I understand vegan or vegetarian diets aren't for everyone, and some options aren't even the healthiest. Noticing Nutrition Facts on what I’m eating, as well as other products that are being pushed to the masses, I realize how difficult it can be to source great options at a reasonable price.

Premium products come at a premium price, however, I believe our standard has dropped. Why settle for the bio solutions (BS) they infect our food with? Order a Llama, and watch wellness take over. Together, we can climb to the top, one step at a time.
